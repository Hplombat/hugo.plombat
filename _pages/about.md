 I'm a postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Michigan. 

 I'm working on tides. 


### My past and present in academia

- **Since November 2024:** Postdoc at the University of Michigan in the [Earth and Environmental Sciences department](https://lsa.umich.edu/earth), working with [Brian Arbic](https://arbic.earth.lsa.umich.edu/).
- **October 2021 - October 2024:** PhD stuent at the [Laboratoire Univers et Particules de Montpellier](https://www.lupm.in2p3.fr/en/homepage/), Université de Montpellier. My thesis was entitled [Investigating the 21cm hydrogen line as cosmological probe of the dark ages and cosmic dawn](https://ged.scdi-montpellier.fr/florabium45/servlet/DocumentFileManager?source=ged&document=ged:IDOCS:1212080&resolution=MEDIUM&recordId=theses%3ABIU_THESE%3A6996&file=PLOMBAT_2024_archivage.pdf&X_CSRF_TOKENS=baMpkSIEaPy3uY0BE1c68TYsV4LOi2GfdcN0sIrf7auXwDY7gJ0hgWSlj3KkryI8), supervised by Denis Puy.
- **September 2016 - June 2021:** Undergraduate student in Fundamental Physics and Master [Cosmos, Fields and Particles](https://formations.umontpellier.fr/fr/formations/master-XB/master-physique-fondamentale-et-applications-ME155/cosmos-champs-et-particules-ccp-PR503.html) at the Universté de Montpellier.
